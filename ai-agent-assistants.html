Common Questions

Is this a one-time cost or monthly?

This is the one-time development price. However, we do offer & recommend our $500/m maintence plan, which includes the latest upgrades & immediate bug fixes.

How do you ensure the quality of your projects?

Our custom AI Agents have a minimum 2-week live testing period, this ensures  you are comfortable with the system and that any issues are resolved quickly.

What is your development process like?

We provide a very transparent development process. This will include weekly update calls to ensure we are consitent with your expectations.

Does this connect with my CRM?

Yes, we will build a custom automation to your AI Agent that can send or retreive data from your CRM.

How long does it take?

Our base package is a 30 day program, this consists of 2-weeks of development and 2-weeks of testing. Depending on the complexity of your Agent this timeframe can increase.

How often will I receive updates on the project?

During the development process you will receive weekly updates. If you choose to accept our maintence plan and we will monitor your system at all times.
