How do AI Chatbots work?

AI chatbots use NLP to interpret human input, leveraging vector space models or knowledge graph embeddings to retrieve information from a company's knowledge base. The RAG technique combines document retrieval and generation for more accurate responses, while key processes like entity extraction and intent classification ensure relevant replies.

How many different services can a chatbot use at the same time?

AI chatbots can connect with multiple services simultaneously, including APIs, communication platforms, databases, and NLP engines to improve user interactions.

What is use case of AI-Powered Chatbots ?

AI-powered chatbots are used for virtual assistants, advanced customer service, and personalized recommendations. They enhance user experience with human-like language, context awareness, and a more natural feel. They scale well and adapt to new queries without constant updates. For example, e-commerce chatbots offer personalized shopping advice.

Can I Analyze Conversations that Take Place with My Chatbot?

Conversations with AI chatbots can be analyzed through conversation transcript dashboard. You can read these transcripts to know how the chatbot is interacting with humans and they can be used to notice patterns and issues the chatbot might experience.
